Education & Awareness Campaigns

Educate Users on Responsible Technology Use

1. Environmental Sustainability

- Informing users of the impact of their technology habits
- Encouraging users to lower power consumption through adjusting device settings and unplugging devices when not in use, to reduce overall carbon footprint
- Emphasizing the importance of responsible recycling and properly disposing of old electronics
- Being mindful of AI use and use it more sustainably, avoiding unnecessary prompts

2. Health

- Promoting proper screen time management and importance of taking breaks
- Informing users of ways to maintain proper posture
- Teach users to establish boundaries to prevent digital burnout
- Emphasis on avoiding blue light before sleep to prevent messing up circadian rhythms
- Encouraging users to spend longer periods of time away from screens and promote offline activities
- Take time to disconnect from social media
