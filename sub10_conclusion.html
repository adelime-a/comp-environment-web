How You Can Contribute to the Environment

There are plenty of non-profit organizations that are dedicated to making sure end-of-life electronics are handled in a safe, secure and environmentally-sound manner. You can refer to search for electronics recycle locations and instructions near your area.

In Ontario, organizations such as the Electronic Products Recycling Association (EPRA) provide accessible drop-off locations where electronic waste can be safely recycled instead of ending up in landfills. There are over 3,100 EPRA drop-off locations across the country. Over 1.4 Million tonnes of electronics have been diverted from Canadian landfill and illegal export through EPRA. EPRA also only work with certified recyclers to make sure electronics are properly recycled.

Take Action:

- Drop off your old electronics at a recycling station.
- Reuse, resell, and donate electronics.
- Repair devices insteand of replacing them immediately.
- Reduce energy use by turning off and unplugging devices when not in use.
- Avoid unnecessary upgrades when devices still work properly.
