Paperless Workplace

Why Go Paperless & How

1. Benefits of Paperless Workplace

Better Organization

Cost Reduction

Environment

- Eliminate time spent searching for misplaced files/sorting through file cabinets
- Allows multiple people to work on shared documents at the same time without the need to pass around paperwork
- Reduces space required for filing cabinets

- Reduces number of printed documents/paperwork
- Working remotely and eliminating filing cabinets optimizes office space efficiency allowing for more collaborative zones and less real estate required

- Reducing paper waste lowers companies' carbon footprint and decreases deforestation
- Around 300 000 metric tons of paper is used worldwide every day, the average North American office worker uses around 2 lbs of paper every day

2. Strategies to Go Paperless

- Set up a cloud storage folder(Microsoft OneDrive, Google Drive, etc.)
- Upload existing paper documents to the folder
- Encourage use of electronic communication, collaboration through digital platforms, and digital document sharing
- Training employees to use digital tools and provide guidance on using new software efficiently
- Gradually implement paperless initiatives for easier adaptation
- Invest in safety software to protect sensitive information
