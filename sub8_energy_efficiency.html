Energy Efficiency Practices

How to Use Energy More Efficiently

Energy-efficient practices help reduce electricity consumption, minimize environmental impact and also lower electricity bills.

Using Energy Efficient Hardware:
- Reducing electricity bills
- Minimize energy lost as heat, generating less excess heat and protecting the battery
- Reducing greenhouse gas emissions

Enabling Power-Saving Modes:
- Extended battery life for laptops,tablets and smartphones, reducing e-waste
- Automatic management of energy consumption
- Does not require user to shut down device

Turning Off Devices When Not in Use:
- Lowered electricity bills
- Reducing wasted energy from devices on standby
- Reduces demand for electricity
